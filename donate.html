Privacy Policy

Global Helping Hands for Children (G2H4C) respects your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or share donor information with any third parties. Personal information collected during the donation process is used solely to process your donation and provide a receipt.

All payment data is securely encrypted and transmitted directly to our payment processor. We do not store any credit card numbers or sensitive financial data on our servers.

Refund Policy

All donations made to Global Helping Hands for Children (G2H4C) are final. If a donation was made in error, please contact us within 7 days and our team will review the request on a case-by-case basis.

Secure Donation Processing

All donations are transmitted through SSL-encrypted HTTPS connections. Look for the padlock icon in your browser’s address bar to confirm your information is secure.
